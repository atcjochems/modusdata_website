About Modus Data

Modus Data is a Swiss company based at Benkenstrasse 3, 4106 Therwil. Founder Arthur Jochems is a senior Data Scientist and AI Engineer with a PhD in Neuroscience and more than a decade of experience across pharma, health, government and telecommunications.

His work includes LLM optimization for Roche, Retrieval-Augmented Generation systems, AI-enabled drug data pipelines, fraud detection, and enterprise AI production with clients such as Daiichi Sankyo.
