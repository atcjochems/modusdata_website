### Academic Rigor Meets Enterprise Execution

My background bridges deep academic research and high-stakes corporate environments. After completing my PhD and working as a postdoctoral researcher in multimodal oncology learning—analyzing complex, messy layers of clinical trial imagery, radiomics, and patient data—I realized my true passion lay in building practical, industrial-grade systems. 

I transitioned into full-time freelance consulting to give companies direct access to elite, specialized AI execution without the agency overhead. 

### My Engineering Philosophy

* **Code Over Hype:** I don't sell generic AI platitudes. I deliver customized codebases, optimized model weights, and transparent infrastructure.
* **Production-First Mindset:** A model that cannot be efficiently deployed, monitored, and scaled is a technical liability. Every architecture I select is chosen with MLOps and long-term maintenance in mind.
* **Domain Expertise:** Having spent over ten years navigating the tightly regulated environments of pharmaceuticals, medtech, and telecom, I build solutions that respect data governance, compliance, and strict validation requirements.

When I'm not configuring GPU workloads or optimizing embedding pipelines, you'll usually find me building custom PC hardware or working out with heavy kettlebells in my home gym.
